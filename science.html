Science PBL

Project-Based Learning (PBL) in a science classroom immerses students in real-world scientific challenges. Through hands-on exploration and collaborative inquiry, they tackle topics like environmental sustainability and technological innovation. By conducting research and proposing solutions, students develop critical thinking skills and scientific literacy, preparing them to address complex global issues as responsible citizens.
