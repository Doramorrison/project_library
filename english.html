English PBL

Project-Based Learning (PBL) in an English class helps students learn by working on real-life problems using English. They choose topics, research them, and then present their findings. This method helps them use English in a practical way, improving their vocabulary and how well they communicate. PBL also teaches teamwork, problem-solving, and creativity as students work together and share their ideas. Ultimately, it prepares them to use English confidently in everyday life and future careers.
