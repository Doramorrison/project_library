Title: Life Before and After Technology
Made by: Year9 Indigo
Group members: Hsu Htet Sandi, Poe Nadi Min, Aw Thu Chal

Click here to view the PBL slides:View

Description

Life before and after technology represents a dramatic shift in how we live. Before technology, communication relied on traditional methods like letters and landline phones, and information was sourced from books and newspapers. Social interactions were more localized, and entertainment was centered around physical activities. With technology, particularly the internet and smartphones, our lives have become more interconnected and convenient. We now have instant access to information, global communication, and digital tools that have transformed work and entertainment. However, this digital revolution has also brought challenges like information overload and increased screen time. Balancing the benefits and drawbacks of technology is essential as we navigate modern life and strive to preserve meaningful human connections.
